About

I’m Antonio Luzano, a visual development artist and illustrator from Montréal, based in Helsinki. I’ve worked on animated TV series, feature productions and children’s books, creating environments, props, colour keys and more.

I love building atmospheric worlds, playful cities and quiet, emotional moments.
