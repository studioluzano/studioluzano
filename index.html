Studio Luzano Koulu · Observation Drawing Basics

I teach beginner-friendly drawing classes in my studio in Kruununhaka, Helsinki. We focus on learning to see like an artist, noticing shapes, light, and proportions, in a calm and relaxed environment. No experience needed. Just curiosity!

About

I’m a Montréal-born concept artist and illustrator now working in Helsinki, with a decade of experience in animation, publishing, and visual storytelling. I’m known for adapting to many styles and bringing ideas to life across TV productions, children’s books, and graphic novels.
