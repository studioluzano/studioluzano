About

I’m Antonio Luzano, a Montréal-born illustrator and concept artist now based in Helsinki. I’ve spent over a decade creating artwork for animated TV series, children’s books, visual development, and publishing. My work focuses on expressive characters, atmospheric environments, and thoughtful storytelling.

I’ve collaborated with studios and publishers in Canada, Finland, and abroad, and I’m always excited to explore new visual challenges — whether through animation, picture books, or personal projects.


Read a bit more

Originally from Montréal, Canada, I moved to Helsinki to continue developing my artistic career and to build Studio Luzano, where I create illustration, concept art, and teach observational drawing. Throughout my work, I try to balance imagination with emotional clarity — whether designing worlds for animation or illustrating quiet, intimate stories for young readers.
