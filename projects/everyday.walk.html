Everyday Walk

A small series of illustrations about quiet walks, small discoveries and the mood of moving through a city on foot. Everyday Walk explores light, colour and atmosphere more than plot — small scenes that feel like memories you pass by.

Evening light on the way home, when everything feels a little slower.
